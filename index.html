Hi, I'm Eileen. I'm a biologist committed to sustainability & biodiversity conservation.

    I grew up in Argentina, lived in Ireland and I´m currently based in Germany.

    My work is driven by a strong belief that the solution to today’s most pressing environmental challenges involves incorporating sustainability into mainstream cultures and redefining our relationship with nature.

    I currently work as a coordinator for biodiversity and sustainability projects and outreach programmes in Ireland. 
    When I’m not working, I’m most likely volunteering in my local bats’ group – nursing baby bats at home – or enabling sustainability in my daily life – whether it is learning to grow and forage food, formulate personal care products or reduce my consumption footprint.
