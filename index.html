Hi, I'm Eileen Elli Ronayne. I'm a biologist committed to conservation and sustainability.

I'm especially interested in: the interplay among top-down and bottom-up cultures in shaping a sustainable future, today's ecological crisis, our relationship with nature, alternative living lifestyles and bats.

Please reach out if any of this sounds interesting to you, if you need help with anything related to the topics above, or if you have a good resource to recommend.
